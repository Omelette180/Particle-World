📖 Tutorial

Controls

  • Left click — draw element  |  Right click — erase
  • Scroll wheel / Arrow keys — brush size
  • Space — pause  |  . (dot) — step one frame
  • + / - — simulation speed  |  H — heat map

Physics

  • Weight affects fall speed — mercury crashes down, snow drifts slowly
  • Heat spreads through metal fast, wood slow, rubber barely at all
  • Ice melts → water → steam as temperature rises
  • Overheated batteries explode

Ocean World

  • Seawater + coral + kelp creates a living ecosystem
  • Sharks hunt fish and crabs  |  Whales drift and blow bubbles
  • Radiation harms coral and fish

Chemistry Lab

  • Draw beaker tiles to build containers, pour Chem-A/B/C inside
  • A+B → Fire  |  A+C → Acid  |  B+C → Steam
  • Add Catalyst to boost reactions  |  A+B+C together → NUKE
  • Check 📋 Recipes button for full list

City Builder

  • Place roads, houses, shops, offices to build a city
  • Citizens follow home → work → shop → sleep routine
  • Police chase zombies  |  Fire trucks respond to burning buildings

Zombie Mode 🧟

  • Press 🧟 button — zombie waves spawn from screen edges

Alien Invasion 👾

  • Press 👾 button — UFOs appear, beam down, abduct citizens

Logic Gates

  • Wire + Battery powers AND/OR/NOT/Timer chains

Portals & Wormholes

  • Portal A ↔ Portal B teleport particles between them
  • Wormhole ejects particles to a random screen edge
